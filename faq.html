    FREQUENT ASKED QUESTION (FAQ)

  • How often should I go to the gym?

    The frequency of gym visits depends on your fitness goals and your body's capacity to recover. Generally, aim for at least 3-4 sessions per week for overall fitness. If you're targeting specific goals like muscle gain or weight loss, your routine might differ.

  • What should I eat before and after a workout?

    Before a workout, opt for a balanced meal containing carbs and protein about 1-2 hours prior to exercise. Post-workout, focus on replenishing glycogen stores with carbs and aiding muscle repair with protein. A protein-carb snack or meal within an hour after exercising is beneficial.

  • How long should my workouts be?

    It depends on your primary fitness goal. If you're aiming for muscle gain, start with strength training to prioritize it while you're fresh. For overall fitness or weight loss, some prefer starting with cardio to warm up and then move to strength training.

  • How many days should i workout?

    It depends on your primary fitness goal. If you're aiming for muscle gain, start with strength training to prioritize it while you're fresh. For overall fitness or weight loss, some prefer starting with cardio to warm up and then move to strength training.
